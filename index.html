About Me

My name is Alexander Bermudez. I hold a degree in Computer Science from California State University, San Marcos. I have 4 years of total experience as a web developer. I worked as a Student Web Developer at Cal State San Marcos for 1 year, and I am currently a UI Web Developer at California Baptist University, where I've been for the last 3 years.

In my current role, I design, develop, and maintain user interfaces for marketing web portfolios and internal web applications, ensuring alignment with user requirements. I have advanced proficiency in HTML, XML, CSS, JavaScript, and XSLT, and I love creating functional, visually appealing, and user-friendly web experiences.
