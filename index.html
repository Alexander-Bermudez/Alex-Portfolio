About Me

I’m Alexander Bermudez, a passionate and experienced web developer with a degree in Computer Science from California State University, San Marcos. Over the past 4 years, I’ve honed my expertise in front-end development, creating intuitive, visually engaging, and responsive user interfaces. My journey began as a Student Web Developer at Cal State San Marcos, where I gained foundational experience, and for the last 3 years, I’ve been shaping digital experiences as a UI Web Developer at California Baptist University.

In my current role, I lead the design, development, and maintenance of user interfaces for both marketing portfolios and internal web applications, always prioritizing user needs and seamless functionality. My advanced skills in HTML, XML, CSS, JavaScript, and XSLT empower me to bring concepts to life, delivering polished and efficient web solutions. I'm passionate about creating impactful web experiences that are not only functional but also beautiful and user-centered.
